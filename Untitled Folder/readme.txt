      The two datasets used for the Named Entity Recognition task for Sinhala are train.txt for training and test.txt for testing. Here only person names, location names and organization names consider as Named Entities. Each line having two columns, the 1st column consists of word tokens and 2nd column consists of appropriate NE tag.
      Both datasets are formatted in IOB format.
      B-NE = denotes beginning of a Named Entity
      I-NE = denotes middle words of a Named Entity
      O = denotes words outside of a Named Entity
